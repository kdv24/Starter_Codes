My Ping Pong Challenge website

Written with the guidance of Epicodus.

This is the great Ping Pong game.

It's kind of like FizzBuzz, but not...

Here are some things to consider in the game:

  • User must be prompted to enter a number,
  • The page will display every number up to that number,
  • for multiples of three the page prints "ping",
  • for multiples of five the page prints "pong",
  • And for numbers that are multiples of three and five the page prints "ping-pong"!

Remember to make it pretty!
